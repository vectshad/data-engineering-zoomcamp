Question 2: What proportion of trips are paid with credit card?

Answer: b) 26.66%

Options:
a) 16.66%
b) 26.66%  ← CORRECT
c) 36.66%
d) 46.66%

My answer: b

Query used:
SELECT 
    ROUND(100.0 * SUM(CASE WHEN payment_type = 'Credit' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) / COUNT(*), 2) as credit_pct
FROM nyc_taxi.taxi_trips;

Result:
Credit Card Proportion: 26.66%

Payment breakdown:
- CASH: 7,235 trips
- Credit: 2,666 trips (26.66%)
- Cash: 97 trips
- Dispute: 1 trip
- No Charge: 1 trip

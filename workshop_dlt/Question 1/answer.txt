Question 1: What is the start date and end date of the dataset?

Answer: b) 2009-06-01 to 2009-07-01

Options:
a) 2009-01-01 to 2009-01-31
b) 2009-06-01 to 2009-07-01  ← CORRECT
c) 2024-01-01 to 2024-02-01
d) 2024-06-01 to 2024-07-01

My answer: b

Query used:
SELECT 
    MIN(trip_pickup_date_time) as start_date,
    MAX(trip_pickup_date_time) as end_date
FROM nyc_taxi.taxi_trips;

Result:
Start Date: 2009-06-01 18:33:00+07:00
End Date: 2009-07-01 06:58:00+07:00
